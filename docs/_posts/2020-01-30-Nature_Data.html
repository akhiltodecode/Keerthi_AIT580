Who collected the Data

The data is published by the US Department of Education where it created a university scorecard to provide reliable and impartial information about university performance. The Ministry of Education works with a variety of national agencies along with the inclusion of a loan repayment program for student debt

Why did they collect the data?

The feature of publishing these files was to provide the students with sufficient data to make informed decisions when choosing a university. It also served the purpose of improving the clarifications on various queries.

What is the nature of the data given the purpose of the data collection?

The data is publicly available and is intended for students planning higher education. We covered all aspects related to admission rate, for the graduate students to the PELL Grants.

Is there any privacy, quality, or other issues with this data?


Privacy

All the data provided on the website is for the purpose of choice making and there is no major objection or flaw in the privacy of data, as such, it can be made an open - source. Only the information on the loan allotment for the students is debateable.


Quality

Although the data is considered for each year like 2020-2021, there is not many cumulative collections/comparisons of the data as in the admission rate or cumulative salaries etc.


Other Data Issues

Few data points are evaluated as average values, hence, does not provide the independent values
