The major concern for the students attending a college is to decide on various aspects like the college rankings, fees structure etc to match a dream college. As a reference for all the challenges, the US government has designed the datarecords about colleges

To assist you in making the best decision when enrolling in college, various visualizations are created that take into account student admission rates, retention rates, median salaries, and fee structures.

Main-focus

Tuition Fee for in-state and out-state students across Public, Private Non-Profit and Private For-Profit schools and Top 20 schools with median salaries of students after 6 and 10 years of entry

Target Audience

With this analysis in place, high school students, current undergraduate students and parents can make decisions about which school to attend
