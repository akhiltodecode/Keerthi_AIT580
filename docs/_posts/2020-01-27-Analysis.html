Analysis on the whole dataset

Read the College Scorecard dataset and find out its shape. dim(dataset) # the dimension of the whole dataset is 6662 rows, 2989 cols

Analyzing the dataset with around 3000 variables is a tideous task. We'll divide the dataset into 4 subsets

Analysis on school metrics

We considered the following columns from the college dataset to understand school performance: "INSTNM", "CONTROL", "CITY", "STABBR", "LATITUDE", "LONGITUDE", "TUITIONFEE_IN", "TUITIONFEE_OUT", "COSTT4_A"

Structure and Summary of School Metrics dataframe

Now, if we observe the structure of this dataset, all these variables are character type. After removing 10114 Null values from the dataframe, we can dive deep into analysing the dataset

Let's look at the summary of this data

Structure and Summary of School Metrics dataframe after data cleaning

We can notice that there are 3 levels of Control i.e. type of institution(1=Public, 2=Private Non-Profit, 3=Private For-Profit). Hence, more students are enrolled into public schools for their post secondary education

Also, we can notice the maximum, minimum and median values of Tution fee for in-state and out-of-state students and their cost of attendance.

Analysis on student metrics

We considered the following columns from the college dataset to understand student performance: "RET_FT4_POOLED", "RET_PT4_POOLED", "C100_4_POOLED"

Similarly, if we observe the structure of this dataset, all these variables are character type. After removing 14201 Null values from the dataframe, we can dive deep into analysing the dataset

Let's look at the summary of this data

Structure and Summary of Student Metrics dataframe after data cleaning

We can notice that the median retention of students enrolled in full-time are more than the students enrolled in part-time.

Also, we can notice that the 50% of students are lying in the 75% overall completion rate region
