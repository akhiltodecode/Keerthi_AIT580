Procedure

The College Scorecard dataset contain around 3000 variables. Some of the variables were discontinued and Privacy compressed.

After observing the dataset, we categorized the whole dataset into four metrics. They are: School, Student, Admission and Earnings metrics.

Questions: School Metrics

1. How many public and private institutions exist in the United States? What state has the most public and private schools?

2. What is the average tuition for in-state and out-of-state students, as well as the overall average cost of attendance for all programs?

Questions: Student Metrics

1. What is the percentage of full-time and part-time students who stay in school?

2. What is the percentage of students who complete a four-year or less-than-four-year degree?

Questions: Admission Metrics

1. Which institution has the highest admission rate?

2. Is there a high admittance rate at public or private institutions?

3. What are the ACT and SAT average scores for public and private schools?

Questions: Earning Metrics

What are the top 15 colleges with the highest average salaries after 6 and 10 years?
